What we do

Reebo pro­vides mon­i­tor­ing and an­a­lyt­ics that shows you every anom­aly and fail­ure in your smart con­tract as it hap­pens, with the de­tails needed to pri­or­i­tize, iden­tify, re­pro­duce, and fix each is­sue. Each month we will process bil­lions of ex­cep­tions from the most pop­u­lar prod­ucts on the de­cen­tral­ized net­works. Our mis­sion is to help hu­mane tech­nolo­gies to out­per­form ad­dic­tive tech­nolo­gies in a post-pri­vacy world, when use of pub­lic ledgers be­come per­va­sive. We are seek­ing ap­pli­cants with a strong un­der­stand­ing of cryp­toe­co­nom­ics and blockchain pro­to­cols and a gen­uine in­ter­est in this space. If you are some­one that is pas­sion­ate about re­bal­anc­ing in­for­ma­tion asym­me­try in the age of AI-first com­pa­nies, we would love for you to join us.
